Privacy Policy

ConnecTris  |  Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ConnecTris ("the app"), developed by Tristan Abbott Coates ("I", "me"), handles information when you use the app on your iOS device. I take your privacy seriously and aim to be transparent about the data that is collected and why.

1. Information I Collect

I do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information from users of ConnecTris. The app does not require you to create an account, and no personal data is transmitted to any server operated by me.

The app stores game progress data (such as your league points, medals, and win/loss record) locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to me or any third party.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

ConnecTris displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data from your device to serve personalised or non-personalised advertisements. This data may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as the iOS Identifier for Advertisers, or IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • App usage information and interaction with ads

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their own privacy policies. I do not have access to or control over the data AdMob collects. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy and learn about Google's advertising practices at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

You can opt out of personalised advertising on your iOS device by going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track", or by enabling Limit Ad Tracking in your device settings.

3. Children's Privacy

ConnecTris is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact me using the details below and I will take steps to remove that information.

4. Data Retention

Game progress data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete the app or clear the app's data through your device settings. I do not retain any user data on external servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. I encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR in the UK and EU, or CCPA in California). Since I do not collect personal data directly, most data-related requests should be directed to Google regarding AdMob data. For any other privacy concerns, please contact me directly.
